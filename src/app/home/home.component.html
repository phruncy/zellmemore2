One Algorithm, 12 depictions: Cellular automata are systems capable of producing fascinating structures from simple rules.

These mathematical models of self organizing systems evolve by repeatedly applying the same algorithm on themselves. Despite – or due to ? – being so simple, their behaviour is of intriguing complexity. This website invites you to visually explore the principle of elementary cellular automata.
